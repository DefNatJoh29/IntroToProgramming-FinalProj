About Me

I am Nate Johns, a tennis player from River Edge, New Jersey. I compete at a 7 UTR level and regularly challenge myself against strong competition. I play second singles for Bergen Tech and take pride in contributing to my teams success. My game is well-rounded, and I adapt my style depending on the surface and opponent. I have won two tournament titles on clay courts, where patience and strategy are key to my success. I have also earned three titles on hard courts, where I can play a more aggressive and fast-paced game.

I am currently ranked 41 in the state of New Jersey, which motivates me to keep improving. I continue to train and compete with the goal of climbing higher in the rankings and developing every part of my game.
